Syllabus Overview

This course is designed to provide students with a robust foundation in the principles and practices of financial reporting for managerial decision-making. We explore the accounting process from its fundamental logic to the preparation and interpretation of complex financial statements.

  • Understanding the Accounting Identity and the dual-entry system.
  • Analysis of key financial statements: Balance Sheet, Income Statement, and Cash Flow.
  • Transition from Accrual Basis to Cash Basis reporting.
  • Practical application through case studies and digital collaborative tools.
